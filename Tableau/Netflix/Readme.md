# 🎬 Netflix Analysis Dashboard

An interactive Tableau dashboard designed to analyze Netflix's content catalog based on content type, release trends, country contribution, genres, and ratings.

The dashboard provides insights into content distribution patterns, platform growth trends, and regional contributions, supporting media analytics and content strategy decisions.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

The objective of this project is to explore Netflix's catalog data and identify patterns in content availability, growth trends, geographic distribution, and audience targeting through ratings analysis.

By visualizing key dimensions of the dataset, this dashboard helps understand how Netflix's content library has evolved over time and highlights major contributors to its global catalog.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze the distribution of content between Movies and TV Shows.
- Identify content growth trends based on release year.
- Understand country-wise contribution to Netflix's catalog.
- Analyze rating distribution across available titles.
- Provide interactive visualizations for exploring content patterns.

---

## 📂 Dataset

**Dataset:** Netflix Titles Dataset

The dataset includes information such as:

- Title
- Content Type (Movie / TV Show)
- Release Year
- Country
- Genre
- Rating

---

## 🛠 Tools & Techniques

- **Tableau Desktop**
- Bubble Charts for content type distribution
- Line Charts for release year trends
- Geographic Maps for country-level analysis
- Filters and Parameters for interactive exploration
- Data Visualization and Dashboard Storytelling

---

## 📊 Dashboard Components

### 1. Content Type Distribution

- Displays the proportion of Movies and TV Shows available on Netflix.
- Helps understand the platform's content strategy mix.

### 2. Release Year Trends

- Shows how Netflix's content library has expanded over time.
- Highlights periods of significant catalog growth.

### 3. Country Contribution Analysis

- Visualizes geographic distribution of Netflix titles.
- Identifies countries contributing the highest number of titles.

### 4. Rating Distribution

- Analyzes content availability across different rating categories.
- Provides insights into Netflix's target audience segments.

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

- **TV-MA** was the most frequently occurring rating, indicating a strong presence of mature audience content.
- Netflix experienced significant content growth after **2015**, aligning with its global expansion phase.
- The **United States and India** were among the largest contributors to Netflix's content catalog.
- Movies represented a larger share of the catalog compared to TV Shows, showing Netflix's strong focus on film content.

---

## 💼 Business Recommendations

- Netflix can continue investing in regional content production to strengthen its presence in high-contribution markets.
- Content strategy can be optimized by balancing Movies and TV Shows based on audience demand and engagement trends.
- Expanding localized content in emerging markets can help attract new subscribers globally.
- Rating-based content analysis can support better audience segmentation and recommendation strategies.
