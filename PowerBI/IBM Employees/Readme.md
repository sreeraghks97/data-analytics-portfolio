# IBM Employee Data Analysis (Power BI)

## 📌 Overview
Dashboard analyzing IBM workforce demographics and departmental distribution.

## 📂 Dataset
- Source: IBM HR dataset
- Format: CSV/Excel with employee records

## ⚙️ Techniques Used
- Power BI data modeling
- DAX measures for attrition and KPIs
- Interactive charts and slicers

## 📊 Key Insights
- Research & Development had the largest share of employees.
- Male employees slightly outnumbered female employees.
- Life Sciences and Medical were the most common education fields.
