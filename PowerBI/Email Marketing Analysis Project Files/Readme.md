# 📧 Email Marketing Analysis | Power BI

![Dashboard Preview](EmailMarketingAnalysis.jpg)

## 📌 Project Overview

This Power BI dashboard analyzes email marketing campaign data to evaluate customer demographics, enrollment patterns, and campaign engagement. It provides an interactive view of customer segments, enabling users to explore trends across gender, marital status, city, and other demographic attributes.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective

The objective of this project is to analyze email marketing data to understand customer demographics, campaign reach, and enrollment trends. The dashboard helps identify key audience segments and supports data-driven marketing decisions through interactive visualizations.

---

## 📂 Dataset

**Source:** Email Marketing Dataset

The dataset contains customer and campaign-related information, including:

- Customer Demographics
- Gender
- Marital Status
- City
- Enrollment Details
- Campaign Information

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

- Interactive filters for customer segmentation
- Demographic analysis by gender and marital status
- City-wise enrollment analysis
- Dynamic visualizations for campaign exploration
- Interactive dashboard for user-driven analysis

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Skills Used

- Power BI
- Data Cleaning
- Data Modeling
- DAX
- Interactive Dashboards
- Data Visualization
- Slicers & Filters

---

## 🔍 Key Findings

- Male customers represent the largest share of the dataset.
- Couples with children form the most prominent customer segment.
- Hyderabad recorded the highest number of enrollments.
- Interactive filtering enables deeper analysis of customer demographics and enrollment patterns.

---

## ✅ Conclusion

This dashboard provides an interactive analysis of email marketing data by transforming customer and campaign information into meaningful visual insights. It demonstrates the use of Power BI for data modeling, DAX calculations, and dashboard development while helping users explore customer demographics and campaign performance through dynamic filtering.
