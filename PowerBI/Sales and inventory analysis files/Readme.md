# 📦 Sales & Inventory Analysis | Power BI

![Dashboard Preview](Sales%20and%20Inventory%20Analysis%20Dahboard.png)

## 📌 Project Overview

This Power BI dashboard analyzes sales and inventory data to evaluate product performance, sales trends, and customer purchasing patterns. It provides an interactive view of product-level metrics, enabling users to identify top-performing products and explore sales distribution across different categories.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective

The objective of this project is to analyze sales and inventory data to identify high-performing products, understand purchasing trends, and evaluate sales performance through interactive visualizations. The dashboard supports data-driven decision-making by providing a comprehensive overview of product sales and revenue.

---

## 📂 Dataset

**Source:** Sales & Inventory Dataset

The dataset contains transaction and product-related information, including:

- Product Name
- Sales Revenue
- Quantity Sold
- Customer Purchases
- Discounts
- Inventory Details

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

- Interactive filters for sales analysis
- Product-wise sales performance
- Revenue comparison across products
- Quantity sold analysis
- Customer purchasing trend visualization
- Dynamic charts for interactive exploration

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Skills Used

- Power BI
- Data Cleaning
- Data Modeling
- DAX
- Interactive Dashboards
- Data Visualization
- Slicers & Filters

---

## 🔍 Key Findings

- Asparagus and Rhubarb recorded the highest sales volume among the available products.
- Strawberry generated the highest overall sales revenue.
- Carrot and Rhubarb achieved the highest quantities sold.
- Interactive filtering enables users to compare product performance, revenue, and purchasing trends across the dataset.

---

## ✅ Conclusion

This dashboard transforms sales and inventory data into an interactive analytical solution that highlights product performance, revenue distribution, and purchasing trends. It demonstrates the use of Power BI for data modeling, DAX calculations, and dashboard development while enabling users to explore business performance through dynamic visualizations.
